### Q: Give an example of inheritance
**A:** "We have an abstract Account class with common properties like balance and accountNumber. SavingsAccount and CurrentAccount inherit from it but implement different interest rates - 4% for savings and 2% for current accounts."

### Q: What about polymorphism?
**A:** "The calculateInterest() method behaves differently in SavingsAccount and CurrentAccount. Same method name, different implementations - that's polymorphism!"
